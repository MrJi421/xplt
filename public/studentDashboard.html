Welcome to Xploit '24

Xploit '24 is an exciting event that brings together students to showcase their skills and participate in various challenges. Get ready for an unforgettable experience filled with learning and competition.

Prelims
(MCQs)
Test your knowledge with multiple-choice questions in the preliminary round.
Semifinal
(CTF)
Compete in Capture The Flag challenges to advance to the finals.
Final
(Escape Room)
Solve puzzles and escape in the final round to be crowned the champion.

Guidelines

  • Ensure you are familiar with the event rules and regulations.
  • Prepare all necessary materials and resources in advance.
  • Follow the event schedule and participate in all rounds.
  • Contact the event coordinators for any queries or issues.
