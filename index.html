About Xploit

•••

Welcome to SYNCHROTECH 2024 Xploit Welcome to SYNCHROTECH 2024 Xploit, the premier cybersecurity event where the brightest minds converge to tackle exhilarating challenges! This high-stakes competition kicks off with a Qualification Round, where teams of two will test their knowledge through a rigorous online quiz on core cybersecurity concepts. The journey continues with the Capture The Flag (CTF) round, where participants will dive into complex scenarios involving web exploitation, cryptography, and binary analysis under intense pressure. The grand finale is the Escape Room challenge, where teams must solve intricate cybersecurity puzzles to "escape" a simulated crisis. With each round designed to escalate in complexity, Xploit promises an action-packed experience that pushes the limits of skill and teamwork, celebrating the passion and prowess of cybersecurity enthusiasts everywhere.

Event Overview

•••

General Guidelines

  • Teams: 2 members
  • Format: Three rounds of escalating difficulty
  • Focus: Computational Studies, Tagline & Logo ID
  • Rules: No malpractice

Rounds

Round 1: Qualification (MCQs)

Format: Online Quiz

Topics: Basics of cybersecurity, networking, and threat analysis

Duration: 30 minutes (30 Questions)

Selection: Top 15-20 teams advance

Round 2: Capture The Flag

Format: Team-based Challenge

Topics: Web exploitation, cryptography

Duration: 1.5 hours (10 Questions)

Selection: Top 8-10 teams proceed

Round 3: Escape Room

Format: Virtual Escape Room

Objective: Solve puzzles to "escape"

Duration: 2 hours

Rules: Hints reduce time; fastest escape wins
